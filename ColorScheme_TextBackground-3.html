Color Scheme Analyzer

ℹ️ Features & References ▼

Features

  • Color Scheme Preview: Visualize combinations in sans-serif and serif fonts.
  • Three Color Specification: CIELAB, LCh or sRGB input. Auto-detects out of sRGB gamut.
  • Vibrating Boundaries: Detects potential Color Assimilation/Spreading.
  • Color Harmony Assessment: Analyzes harmony based on lightness, chroma, and hue angles.
  • Spatial Effect: Predicts whether text advances or recedes based on depth perception.
