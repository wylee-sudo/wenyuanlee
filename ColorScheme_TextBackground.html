Color Scheme ： text-background color combination

Features

  • Color Scheme Preview: Visualize text-background color combinations with "Color Design" displayed in both sans-serif and serif fonts.
  • Three Color Specification: Input colors using CIELAB, LCh or sRGB. The system automatically detects and warns if colors fall outside the sRGB gamut.
  • Vibrating Boundaries（or Color Vibration) Detection: Automatically detects potential Color Assimilation/Spreading.
  • Color Harmony Assessment: Analyzes the harmony between two colors based on their lightness, chroma, and hue angles.
  • Spatial effect assessment: Analyzes the perceived depth of two colors to predict whether the text color will advance or recede.
