About Us

We are dedicated to advancing knowledge in color science, color emotion, harmony, and interaction design. Our lab bridges engineering and design, exploring both the technical foundations of color systems and their applications in digital media, UI/UX, and cultural expression.

Research Areas

  • Color Emotion & Harmony in Design
  • Graphical User Interface & Experience Design (GUI/UX)
  • Virtual Identity & Avatars

Leader of Color & Design Research Lab

Wen-Yuan LEE received his Ph.D. in Color Science from the University of Leeds, UK, in 2007. He is currently a Professor in the Department of Digital Media Design at Tatung University, Taipei, Taiwan.

His research interests encompass design thinking, color emotion, color harmony, and graphical user interface/experience (GUI/UX) design. He teaches courses in digital product design, interaction design, color design, and user experience design. With a strong background bridging color science and interaction design, he is dedicated to advancing the integration of aesthetics, technology, and human-centered innovation.
