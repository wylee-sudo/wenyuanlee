Color & Design Research Lab

Exploring Color, Design, User Experience, and Computational Aesthetics

Leader of Color & Design Research Lab

Professor Wen-Yuan Lee is currently a faculty member in the Department of Digital Media Design at Tatung University in Taipei, Taiwan. His research interests encompass design thinking, color design, user experience, and computational aesthetics. He teaches courses in digital design, interaction design, color design, and user experience design. Professor Lee is dedicated to advancing the integration of aesthetics, technology, and human-centered innovation.

Research Areas

  • Color Emotion & Harmony in Design
  • Graphical User Interface & Experience Design (GUI/UX)
  • Virtual Identity & Avatars
